How to Play

Safety:Safety first! Make sure nobody gets physically or emotionally hurt!

Secrecy:What happens in the game stays in the game! Nobody is allowed to tell anyone about the things that happened during playing.

Skiping:Let's be honest, it can happen to anyone. Someone asks an embarrassing question or demands a hard dare, and you just want to skip it. It's a game and everyone should feel comfortable while playing. So decide what a player has to do if they want to skip a turn. For Example: Dance, Sing, Take a Shot, etc.

Quiting:Before you and your friends start playing, you should decide on a punishment for anyone who quits the game. For example: Calling there crush, confess his/her love, etc.
